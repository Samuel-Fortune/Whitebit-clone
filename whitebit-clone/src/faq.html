Registration and account setup

How to sign up on WhiteBIT?

In order to start trading and using the full range of tools of our exchange, you should create an account on WhiteBIT.

The registration process takes only a few minutes. Here is how to follow it.

  • Follow the link https://whitebit.com/auth/register or click Sign Up in the top right corner of the website.
  • Enter your email address and a strong password in the correspondent fields. A password must be at least 8 characters long and contain at least 1 lowercase letter, 1 uppercase one, 1 number and 1 special character.
  • You can also add a referrer ID: just press the "+" sign to view an input field.
  • Check the boxes agreeing to the WhiteBIT User Agreement, Privacy Policy and confirming that you are neither a citizen nor a resident of prohibited countries and territories.
  • Press the Continue button.
  • Complete CAPTCHA.
  • Confirm your email by entering the verification code sent to your email. If you do not receive a code within a minute, you can send a new letter.
  • Use your credentials to log in to your account.

To ensure the extra security of your account, we recommend that you activate two-factor authentication (2FA) and anti-phishing.

Enjoy all the benefits of our exchange and earn together with WhiteBIT!

How to login to your account?

The Log In button is located in the top right corner of the website.

Click it and you will be redirected to the login page.

  • Make sure you are on https://whitebit.com .
  • Enter your email address and password, then complete CAPTCHA. If you have the 2FA function activated, enter a code as well and click Continue.
  • Note that if 2FA on your account is not activated and you try to log in from a new device, you will have to enter a code sent to your email address. This increases the security of your account.

That is all! You will be automatically redirected to the Basic Trade page.

Balance

After registration on WhiteBIT exchange, each user has access to 4 types of Balance pages: Total, Main, Trade and Collateral.

When you make a deposit, all the assets go to your Main Balance. To start trading, you need to transfer them to your Trade Balance (or Collateral Balance if you want to start margin or futures trading). These balances are explicitly created to separate the assets and make the process of trading even more comfortable.

Total balancecontains general information of all balances, it is always shown in USDT, as an equivalent the value is displayed in BTC. In the total balance section, you can make deposits and withdrawals, and if deposits/withdrawals of a certain currency is closed, it is possible to subscribe/unsubscribe in order to receive notifications when they are available again.

Main balanceis for depositing and withdrawing cryptocurrency and national currency. If you have balance on your account, you can create a code and open SMART Staking plan on the very same page (only with the coins/tokens available for plan opening).

You can find your funds on the Main balance:

  • after depositing;
  • before withdrawing;
  • after interest for referrals accrued;
  • before creating a WhiteBIT code;
  • after activating a WhiteBIT code;
  • before opening a SMART Staking plan;
  • after a SMART Staking Plan is closed or ended.

Trade balance is intended solely for non-marginal trading (placing orders), as well as for the currency exchange.

There are 3 buttons in the line to the right of an asset:

  • "Trade": to go to the trading page;
  • "Transfer": to make a transfer between balances;
  • "Exchange": to quickly exchange currencies at the market price.

In order to start trading, you need to transfer the necessary amount of funds from the Main balance to the Trade balance. Using the “History” section next to the “Buy crypto” button, you can check the history of your trading transactions.

Collateral balance - total balance for margin and futures trading. The currencies only available for margin and futures trading are displayed. In order to start trading with 1x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 10x or 20x leverage or use futures trading, you need to transfer the necessary amount of funds from the Main or Trade balance to the Collateral balance

Identity verification (KYC)

What is KYC and what is it for?

KYC is the verification of a user, the confirmation of their identity by requesting personal data. It is the abbreviation for “Know Your Customer” or “Know Your Client”.

Before passing identity verification, you can try our Demo Tokens and see how our trading tools work. However, identity verification is required for making any deposits and withdrawals, creating and aсtivating WhiteBIT Codes and using the Buy crypto option.

Also, KYC-verification helps to secure their account and ensure the funds safety. It is worth remembering that online verification indicates the exchange’s reliability. The platform that does not require any data is not liable to you. If you visit a crypto exchange with no identity verification required and hope to buy Bitcoin, take responsibility for possible risks. Besides, identity verification prevents money laundering and financing of terrorism.

How to pass the identity verification?

The process of verification is very simple. Submitting an application only takes a few minutes.

Log in to your account and go to the Verification section in the settings.

The identity verification section is only available to registered users who have logged in, and have not been verified yet.

  • Fill in the personal information.Note that all the required fields should be filled out and the data should exactly correspond to the information in your document.

  • Upload a photo (or photos) of one of the documents proving an identity: a national passport, international passport, national ID Card or driving licence.The picture cannot be cropped or edited.

    Take a selfie with your document and a sheet of paper with handwritten “WhiteBIT.com” and the current verification date.

    Wait for the document check to complete. Once your application has been processed, you will receive a letter via e-mail notifying you of the identity verification check results. Your status in the Verification section will change upon successful completion. If your application is rejected, the letter will also state the reason.

The users from Ukraine can pass simplified verification, which includes only filling out the form and uploading a photo of the document. They also can pass quick verification via the state Diia portal.

We have also created a short video which will help to easily understand the verification process.

Please note that currently we do not accept identity verification from citizens or residents of the following countries and territories: Afghanistan, American Samoa, the U.S. Virgin Islands, Guam, Iran, Yemen, Libya, Palestine, Puerto Rico, Somalia, North Korea, the Northern Mariana Islands, the USA, Syria, Sudan, South Sudan, Transnistria, the temporarily occupied territories of Georgia, Nagorno-Karabakh, Northern Cyprus, Western Sahara, Ambazonia, Kosovo, Republic of Belarus, Russian Federation and the temporarily occupied territories of Ukraine.

How long should I wait for KYC to be checked? What should I do if I made a mistake in the application form?

Identity verification usually takes from 1 to 24 hours. All the requests are processed in order, so please have patience.

After your application is processed, you will receive an email with the information about the result. If your KYC information is disapproved, the email will also include a reason. Moreover, your identity verification status on the Verification page will be changed.

If you made a mistake during the verification process, just wait until the application is disapproved. You will then be able to submit your information for approval again. If you still have any questions, please contact our support team via online chat on the exchange (the icon in the bottom right corner of the site), a ticket or email support@whitebit.com.

Deposit / Withdrawal

Depositing a cryptocurrency

In order to start trading, you need to deposit funds into your account.

Each cryptocurrency asset is deposited separately on the " Total" or "Main" balance page. In the line with the currency ticker, you will see the corresponding "Deposit" and "Withdraw" buttons.

Let's try to make a deposit!

For example, in order to deposit Tether US (USDT), click Balances in the main menu at the top and select " Total" or "Main ". Find the currency by the ticker 'USDT' and click the "Deposit" button. In the deposit form select the network via which the deposit will be made, copy the deposit address (with a button or QR-code) and use it on the sending platform. Please pay attention to the minimum deposit amount and fee.

If you use the mobile application, go to the Wallet section (Exchange mode), find USDT and tap Deposit. Then act in accordance with the previous instructions.

Any deposit into your WhiteBIT account is shown in your history (Deposit tab) and the funds are deposited to your main balance.

Please pay attention to the following important points!

  • In the deposit window, always check the list of networks (token standards, respectively) which are supported on WhiteBIT. You can also check the network explorer of each individual coin by clicking the chain icon next to the ticker.

  • Make sure the network you are going to use for deposit matches the network supported on WhiteBIT.

  • Make sure you copy the deposit address of the correct network.

  • Do not forget about the minimum deposit amount and Fees.

  • Remember that there is a memo (destination tag) for some currencies like Ripple (XRP) and Stellar (XLM). You will see it in the Your Memo field in the deposit window. Entering a memo on a sending platform is a prerequisite for your deposit to be credited.

Be careful, if you enter incorrect information while making a transaction, your assets may be irretrievably lost. Before making transactions, please make sure that the information you enter to send funds to your balance is correct.

Every cryptocurrency transaction requires a certain number of network confirmations; when it is reached, a deposit will be credited to your balance. You can find information about the required numbers of confirmations below, in the Number of Network Confirmations section.

If a deposit does not appear on your balance page please contact our support team by creating a Ticket on the website.

To make the processing easier, it should include:

  • a topic;
  • the e-mail you used to create your account;
  • a problem/question;
  • a transaction hash.

Depositing national currency

Each national currency is deposited separately on the " Total" or "Main" balance page. In the line with the ticker of a coin, you will see the corresponding buttons Deposit and Withdrawal.

Let's try to make a deposit!

In order to deposit US dollars (USD), for example, click Balances in the main menu at the top and then select " Total" or "Main" Find the currency by the ticker 'USD' and click on the Deposit button. In the deposit form, check and select the payment system through which the deposit will be made, enter the amount to be deposited (the fee will be deducted from it) and click Request. You will be automatically redirected to the page for payment through the payment system. Then use the prompts on that page.

If you use the mobile application, go to the Wallet section (Exchange mode), find USD and press Deposit. Then follow the same steps as in the previous instruction.

Funds transfer is very fast. Pay attention that if the status of your deposit in the history is "Unconfirmed by user", make sure that you have successfully completed all stages of the transaction. If you are sure it is so and your funds were withdrawn but did not reach your balance, please contact our support (via e-mail, chat or ticket) and we will check the information further and provide you with appropriate recommendations.

You can check payment systems, fees and minimum deposits on the Fees page.

Withdrawing a cryptocurrency

Each cryptocurrency asset is withdrawn separately on the "Total" or " Main " balance page. In the line with the ticker of a coin, you will see the corresponding buttons Deposit and Withdrawal.

Let's try to make a withdrawal!

In order to withdraw Tether US (USDT), for example, click Balances in the main menu at the top and then select "Total" or " Main ". Find the currency by the ticker 'USDT' and click on the Withdrawal button. In the withdrawal form, select the network through which the withdrawal will be made and insert the address where the funds will be sent. Please pay attention to the minimum withdrawal amount and the fee (you can choose whether you would like to deduct or add the fee to the entered amount by the toggle). Then click the Withdrawal Request button. You will have to confirm the withdrawal via email as well as 2FA (if the function is enabled).

If you use the mobile application, go to the Wallet section (Exchange mode), find USDT and tap Withdrawal. Then follow the same steps as in the previous instructions.

Any withdrawal from your WhiteBIT account is displayed in your history (Withdrawals tab) and funds are withdrawn from the main balance.

Please pay attention to the following important points!

  • In the withdrawal window, always check the list of networks (token standards, respectively) that are supported on WhiteBIT and make sure that the network through which you are going to withdraw is supported on the receiving side. You can also check the network explorer of each individual coin by clicking on the chain icon next to the ticker.
  • Make sure that you enter the correct withdrawal address of the corresponding network.
  • Remember that a memo (destination tag) can be required for some currencies on a receiving platform. Filling in the memo correctly is a prerequisite for your funds to be successfully credited. However, if a memo is not required on a receiving platform, just enter '12345' in the corresponding field.

Be careful, if you enter incorrect information while making a transaction, your assets may be irretrievably lost. Before each transaction, please make sure that the information you enter to withdraw funds from your balance is correct.

All withdrawals usually take from one minute to one hour to be completed. There may be exceptions if a network is overloaded. If you have any difficulty while withdrawing, please contact our support (via email, chat or ticket) and we will further verify the information and provide you with appropriate recommendations.

When you deposit such funds, you should specify not only an address (Our Account), but also Your Destination Tag on the WhiteBIT exchange. Otherwise, a deposit will not be credited to the balance.

  • "Trade": to go to the trading page;

  • "Exchange": to quickly exchange currencies at the market price.

Collateral balance - total balance for margin and futures trading. The currencies only available for margin and futures trading are displayed. In order to start trading with 1x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 10x or 20x leverage or use futures trading, you need to transfer the necessary amount of funds from the Main or Trade balance to the Collateral balance

  • You will receive a letter to your email, open it and go through a link to the password change form.
  • Create a new strong password and enter it.

Done!
