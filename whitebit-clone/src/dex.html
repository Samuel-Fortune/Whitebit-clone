FAQ

What is WhiteSwap?
WhiteSwap decentralized exchange (DEX) that currently works on the Ethereum and Tron blockchains. The platform is the product of WhiteBIT.

What is a governance token?
A governance token is a token that allows its holder to participate in the management of a cryptocurrency project. The governance token of WhiteSwap is WSD.

How does WhiteSwap work?
The platform has no orderbook or central facilitator of trade. The pools are defined by a smart contract. It includes a number of functions that enable token swapping, adding liquidity, etc.
