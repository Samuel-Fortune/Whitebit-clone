Any Questions?

What is WhiteBIT Earn?

WhiteBIT Earn is a product family from the WhiteBIT cryptocurrency exchange. With the help of WhiteBIT Earn, you can earn on lending or staking crypto.

What is currently available from WhiteBIT Earn products?

You can now use the Crypto Lending product. It allows you to make profit by lending your funds to the exchange in a certain cryptocurrency. In the future, we plan to launch Staking.

What is a Crypto Lending?

Cryptocurrency lending is a loan to the exchange of certain crypto assets for a certain period at a certain percentage (lending rate).

Is Crypto Lending safe?

Yes. The WhiteBIT exchange stores 96% of digital assets in cold wallets and uses WAF to detect and block hacker attacks. Your funds will be safe for the entire deposit period.

Can I invest in Staking?

Cryptocurrency Staking is still in the development stage, but you can subscribe to the newsletter and be notified when Staking goes live on WhiteBIT.

How does Crypto Staking work?

If you stake crypto, your assets participate in the Proof-of-Stake process, that is, they provide verification and protection of transactions. And you receive a reward in the form of interest or a fixed amount.
